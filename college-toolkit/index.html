Most students use college planning tools in isolation. They calculate their GPA on one site, search for scholarships on another, and read college profiles on a third. That fragmented approach wastes time and leads to missed opportunities.

The Tutela College Toolkit is designed so that each tool feeds into the next:

Starting with the GPA Calculator and finishing with the Alumni Network gives you a complete picture in one session. That is what most students, doing it alone, take months to piece together.

GPA Requirements for US University Scholarships: A Quick Reference

One of the most common questions Indian students have is: what GPA do I actually need to qualify for scholarships at US universities? The GPA Calculator and Scholarship Calculator in the Toolkit are built to answer exactly that. Here is a general reference to calibrate your expectations:

GPA on 4.0 Scale Scholarship Tier Typical Universities
3.9 to 4.0 Full merit scholarships, Presidential awards Alabama, Arizona State, Tulsa, Rochester
3.7 to 3.89 Significant merit aid, 50% to 75% tuition coverage Northeastern, Case Western, Fordham
3.5 to 3.69 Partial merit scholarships, need-based aid eligible Boston University, Syracuse, Pepperdine
3.2 to 3.49 Basic merit consideration, need-based focus Wide range of mid-tier US universities
Below 3.2 Need-based only at most schools Community colleges, specific programs

These ranges are indicative. The Scholarship Calculator inside the toolkit uses your exact GPA, test scores, major, and target country to give you a personalised match, not just a generic range. Use that for accurate eligibility estimates.

What Makes a Good College Navigator Tool

The phrase 'College Navigator' refers to any tool that helps students search, filter, and compare colleges based on their personal criteria. The original College Navigator was built by the US National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) and covers over 7,000 US postsecondary institutions.

Tutela's College Navigator builds on that foundation and tailors it specifically for international students from India. Here is what sets it apart:

  • Coverage across US, UK, Canada, and Singapore, not just US institutions
  • Major-specific search so you can find every university that offers Computer Science, Economics, Engineering, or any other program
  • Admissions data contextualised for international applicants, including international acceptance rates where available
  • Integration with the Scholarship Calculator so you can see financial aid options while browsing colleges
  • A dashboard that saves your searches and lets you build a comparison list across sessions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the College Toolkit completely free?

Yes. All four tools are accessible for free. The GPA Calculator is fully free with no restrictions. The Scholarship Calculator and College Navigator give you one free search each. The Alumni Network is free for a limited period. Register on the Tutela platform to activate your free access.

How does the GPA Calculator convert Indian grades to a 4.0 scale?

The GPA Calculator uses standard conversion tables recognised by US and UK universities to translate percentage scores and grade points from CBSE, IB, IGCSE, ISC, A-Level, and Indian state board systems into the 4.0 scale. You enter your grades by subject, and the calculator handles the conversion automatically.

How accurate is the Scholarship Calculator?

The Scholarship Calculator uses real eligibility data from 1,000+ universities and scholarship programs. It matches your profile against merit and need-based criteria and shows you which awards align with your GPA, test scores, and major. Eligibility results are a strong indicator, but always confirm requirements directly with the institution before applying.

How do I find colleges for my major using the College Navigator?

Search by subject in the College Navigator and every university offering that major appears in your results. You can then filter by location, tuition range, acceptance rate, and average GPA to narrow down to schools that fit your academic and financial profile.

Can I reach out to alumni directly through the Alumni Network?

Yes. Once you register and activate your free account, you can browse alumni profiles and send messages directly. Alumni are students who have studied at Tutela and are now enrolled at universities in the US, UK, Canada, and Singapore. They are there to share honest, first-hand experience.

Is the toolkit useful for students in Grade 10?

Absolutely. Students in Grade 10 benefit most from starting early. Use the GPA Calculator to understand where your academic profile currently stands, begin researching colleges with the Navigator, and check what GPA improvements in Grades 11 and 12 could unlock in terms of scholarship eligibility.

Does the toolkit cover UK universities as well?

Yes. The College Navigator and Scholarship Calculator both cover universities in the UK alongside the US, Canada, and Singapore. If you are considering LSE, UCL, Warwick, Edinburgh, or other UK institutions alongside your US list, the toolkit handles both.
