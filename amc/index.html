Comprehensive AMC Prep for Future Math Achievers

Tutela’s AMC preparation courses are designed for ambitious students who wish to excel in mathematics beyond the standard curriculum. The American Mathematics Competitions (AMC) series stimulates mathematical thinking, develops problem-solving skills, and provides early recognition for talented students — making it an ideal addition for those targeting top STEM programmes.

AMC Subject Levels we specialize in

  • AMC 10: 25 questions, 75 minutes; for students in Grade 10 or below (or under 17.5 years old). Covers algebra, basic geometry, number theory, and elementary probability.
  • AMC 12: 25 questions, 75 minutes; for students in Grade 12 or below (or under 19.5 years old). Tests the full high-school curriculum up to pre-calculus (excluding calculus).

Frequently asked Questions

How early should a student start AMC preparation at Tutela?

Ideally, 3-6 months before the competition, depending on your current confidence with maths. An early start maximizes time for concept building, problem practice, and strategy refinement.

Does Tutela provide mock tests for AMC?

Yes, we conduct timed full-length mock AMC tests along with weekly/bi-weekly topic tests. Each test is followed by in-depth performance reviews and targeted revision of weak areas.

Can AMC prep at Tutela help me qualify for further competitions like AIME or USAMO?

A: Absolutely. The AMC 10 and AMC 12 serve as the first step in the invitational pathway (AIME- USAJMO/USAMO). Tutela’s preparation is designed with that pathway in mind.
